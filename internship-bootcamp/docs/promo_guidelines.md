# Promotion Guidelines

## General Rules
Promotions should be clear, fair, and easy for customers to understand.

## Discount Rules
Discounts may be applied based on product category, order amount, or customer segment.

## Example Promotions
- 10% discount on electronics orders above 500 AED.
- Free delivery for orders above 300 AED.
- Buy 2 get 1 free for selected products.

## Restrictions
Promotions cannot be combined unless clearly stated.
Refunded or cancelled orders are not eligible for promotional benefits.
