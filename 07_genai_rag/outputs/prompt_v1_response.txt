Prompt Version 1 — Simple Prompt

Prompt:
Summarize the following customer feedback and highlight the main issues:

1. The delivery was late, but the product quality was good.
2. Customer support took too long to reply to my issue.
3. The app is easy to use and the checkout process is simple.
4. My order arrived damaged and I had to request a replacement.
5. The prices are fair, but delivery tracking was not accurate.
6. I liked the product, but the refund process was confusing.
7. The website design is clear and easy to navigate.
8. I waited several days for customer support to solve my problem.

Generated Response:
The customer feedback is mixed. Customers liked the product quality, fair prices, simple checkout process, and easy-to-use app and website. However, several issues were mentioned, including late delivery, slow customer support, damaged orders, inaccurate delivery tracking, and a confusing refund process.

The main issues are delivery problems, customer support delays, damaged products, and unclear refund processes.

Evaluation Notes:
This prompt gives a basic summary, but it does not force the output to follow a clear structure. It also does not rank the issues or explain which problems are repeated the most.
